Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?
Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree in a quantitative field (mathematics, statistics, computer science, or related) with a minimum GPA of 3.0. Some programs may require prerequisite coursework in calculus, linear algebra, or programming.
Is there an application fee?
Yes, there is a $50 non-refundable application fee. Fee waivers are available for eligible candidates based on financial need or participation in partner programs.
Can I apply for financial aid or scholarships?
Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships, need-based aid, and research assistantships. All admitted students are automatically considered for funding opportunities.
What if English is not my first language?
Non-native English speakers must submit TOEFL (minimum 90 iBT) or IELTS (minimum 6.5) scores. Waivers may be granted for applicants who completed prior education in English.
